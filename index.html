Conflict Resolution

  • Team members in conflict will be asked to attempt to work out the problem among themselves.

  • If this is unsuccessful, they will ask senior team members for input

  • Senior team members will either make a final decision or choose to bring the problem to a vote among senior team members.

  • If this is unsuccessful, the problems will be brought to the Faculty Advisor
